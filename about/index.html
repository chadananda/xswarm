About XSwarm

XSwarm transforms individual developers into AI team leaders, revolutionizing how software is built by orchestrating specialized AI agents that work in parallel to multiply productivity.

Our Mission

We believe every developer deserves to work like they have an entire team at their disposal. XSwarm makes this possible by providing an interactive CLI that orchestrates AI agents specialized in different aspects of software development.

No more context switching between coding, testing, documentation, and deployment. No more feeling overwhelmed by the scope of modern software projects. With XSwarm, you become a team leader, directing AI specialists while you focus on the big picture.

How XSwarm Works

1. Launch the Interactive CLI

Simply run xswarm in your project directory. No commands to memorize, no complex configuration. Just natural conversation with your AI team.

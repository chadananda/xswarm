Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/7/2025

Introduction

xSwarm ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our open-source CLI tool and website.

Information We Collect

CLI Tool

The xSwarm CLI tool is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect any personal data through the CLI tool
  • All processing happens locally on your machine
  • No code, project data, or usage analytics are transmitted to our servers
  • API keys (such as OpenAI keys) are stored locally and never shared with us

Website

On our website (xswarm.ai), we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics through privacy-respecting analytics
  • Information you voluntarily provide (e.g., if you contact us)

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our open-source project
  • Understand how the tool is being used (website analytics only)
  • Respond to your inquiries or support requests

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information we process. However, since xSwarm operates locally on your machine, you maintain control over your data security.

Third-Party Services

xSwarm may interact with third-party services that you configure:

  • AI Providers: When you use AI features, your interactions are governed by your chosen AI provider's privacy policy (e.g., OpenAI)
  • GitHub: Integration with GitHub is subject to GitHub's privacy policy

Open Source Nature

xSwarm is open source software. The source code is publicly available on GitHub, allowing you to verify our privacy practices and ensure no hidden data collection occurs.

Children's Privacy

xSwarm is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Email us at: chadananda@gmail.com
